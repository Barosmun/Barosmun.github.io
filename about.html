About

My name is Barrett Osmundson. I'm a programmer and software developer soon to graduate from the University of Denver, with a B.S. in Computer Science and minors in Mathematics and Political Science. In my free time, I like to build things. I like to try new projects that challenge my experience, so I've dipped my toes into various applications of programming. I'm most passionate about game design and development, which I have been involved in since high school. To me, games are truly the ultimate form of media. I read, listen to music, and watch movies frequently, and I feel that great video games have the potential to encapsulate all the best aspects of these forms of entertainment. It really is incredible. Aside from from writing code, I like to play the drums, study geography and politics, and (of course) spend time with family, friends, and animals.
